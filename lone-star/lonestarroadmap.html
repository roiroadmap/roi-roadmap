03 — The Solutions

What gets built,
and why it matters for you.

01
After-Hours Communication System
Communications

An AI system trained on your properties, your lease terms, and your most common tenant questions handles messages when you're off the clock. Routine stuff gets answered intelligently and immediately. Anything that actually needs you gets escalated — and you decide what that threshold is. The point isn't to replace your judgment. It's so that when you leave for the day, you can actually leave.

⏱ ~5 hrs saved per week on after-hours communication
🧘 Zero late-night interruptions for routine questions
02
5-Minute Lead Response Engine
Leasing — Highest ROI Priority

The second a lead hits Zillow, an outreach goes out — warm, personalized to the unit, and in your voice — in under five minutes. It asks your pre-qualification questions, keeps the conversation moving, and hands you a qualified prospect ready to schedule. You stop losing leases to slow response time and start actually converting the leads you're already generating. This one alone pays for everything else.

🏠 4 leases recovered per quarter at current lead volume
💰 ~$18,400 in recovered annual revenue at $1,150 avg rent
03
Vendor Intelligence & Work Order Loop
Maintenance — Built as One System

Vendor management and work order tracking are built as one integrated system because that's how they actually work in the real world. A request comes in, gets triaged by urgency and trade type, and gets matched to the right vendor from your existing roster based on their history and availability. The system runs the follow-up loop from there — dispatch confirmation, job completion, tenant sign-off. Every job builds a vendor scorecard automatically so over time you know exactly who to call first and who to stop calling.

⏱ ~3 hrs saved per week on vendor coordination and WO follow-up
📈 Full visibility on every open work order, always
04
Owner Reporting & Live Dashboard
Reporting

Your six owner clients get a live dashboard that shows them what they actually want to know — rent collected, open maintenance costs, vacancy status, projected monthly net. Twice a month a plain-English summary goes out automatically. Not a spreadsheet. An actual communication that tells the story of their portfolio that month. Your end-of-month statements pull directly from Buildium and go out on schedule. Three days of manual work becomes thirty minutes of review.

⏱ ~2.5 days recovered every month
🤝 Proactive owner communication without lifting a finger
